❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is a Floor Price?
The floor price is the lowest asking price for any NFT in a collection — essentially the cheapest way to buy in. On Streak Market it's computed trustlessly on Bitcoin L1.
How does OP_NET enable NFTs on Bitcoin?
OP_NET deploys AssemblyScript smart contracts on Bitcoin Layer 1 using the OP-NET protocol. NFTs (OP-721 artifacts) live natively on BTC — no sidechains, no wrapped assets.
What wallet do I need?
Connect using OP_WALLET — the only wallet with full OP_NET and quantum-resistant ML-DSA signature support. Download from the official OP_NET website.
Are fees non-custodial?
Yes. All funds flow through the Streak_Market smart contract on Bitcoin L1. A 2.5% protocol fee is taken on sales — no intermediaries hold your assets.

❓ Lending FAQ
What is APY and how is it calculated?
APY is calculated using a kinked utilization model: baseRate (2%) + slope (10% below 80% util, 50% above). It compounds per Bitcoin block (~10 min).
What collateral is accepted?
Currently: OP-20 tokens and BTC. NFT collateral (OP-721) is in architecture — enabled in the next protocol upgrade via Streak_Lend v2.
What is a Flash Loan?
Flash loans let you borrow any amount with no collateral — as long as you repay within the same Bitcoin block. Used for arbitrage, liquidations, and advanced strategies.
What happens if I'm liquidated?
If your LTV exceeds 80%, liquidators can repay part of your debt and claim your collateral at a 5% discount. Keep LTV below 70% for a safety buffer.

❓ DAO FAQ
How are proposals created?
Any address with non-zero STREAK token balance can submit a proposal on-chain via StreakDAO. After a 10-block delay, voting opens for 144 blocks (~24 hrs).
What is quorum?
A proposal requires 4% of total voting power to participate (quorum). If quorum is met and FOR votes exceed AGAINST, the proposal succeeds after the timelock.
How is the treasury managed?
The StreakDAO treasury holds all protocol revenue on Bitcoin L1. Disbursements require a successful governance vote and timelock execution.
Can I delegate my votes?
Yes — delegate your voting power to any address via the Streak DAO interface. Delegation is on-chain and can be revoked at any time.
